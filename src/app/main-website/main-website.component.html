Early childhood, the period from birth to eight years old, is a time of remarkable growth with brain development at its peak. Nidara-Children aims at the holistic development of a child’s social, emotional, cognitive and physical needs in order to build a solid and broad foundation for lifelong learning and well being.

"This early child development system is a very useful tool for promoting normal development of children and will serve the community well. "

- Dr. Balasubramanian, Medical Director,
Kanchi Kamakoti Childs Trust Hospital

DISCOVER NIDARA-CHILDREN

HEALTH & WELL BEING

A high performing child needs a whole child approach that includes solid brain, body and emotional development.
We help your child form daily routines, get the right nutrition, do yoga and develop social milestones.

PERSONALIZED LEARNING

Every little child is precious and unique. Learning is personal.
We help them to learn in their own way by personalizing your child’s learning experiences at their own pace ability as per the most rigor standards set by professional organizations in the United States and Europe.

INTEREST EXPLORATION

Every child has a talent that is inherent in them. Help your child explore their interests and nurture their curiosity..
We give your little one an opportunity to explore various themes to begin their exciting journey.

SPEND QUALITY TIME

Fill your little child’s early years with love.

With our daily parent tips based on your child’s daily session with Nidara engage with your child meaningfully.

TRACK DEVELOPMENT

Raising children as per their milestones is important.
Keep track of how your child is doing health wise, how they are learning and what interests they are exploring.
